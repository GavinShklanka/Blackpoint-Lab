# Blackpoint Architecture Lab Research and experimentation in governance-aware AI systems and institutional decision infrastructure. Blackpoint Lab explores architectures for deploying intelligent systems inside complex operational environments such as healthcare, finance, education, and public infrastructure. The focus of this work is not simply prediction models, but systems capable of **routing decisions, enforcing governance constraints, and integrating human oversight**. --- # Research Focus Modern institutions rarely suffer from lack of prediction. They suffer from poorly structured **decision pathways**. Signals emerge across fragmented systems and must be evaluated across multiple intervention options. Blackpoint Lab explores architectures capable of: • Decision routing • Agent orchestration • Governance middleware • Human-in-the-loop oversight • Institutional observability --- # Architecture Ecosystem The following systems explore different components of institutional AI infrastructure. --- ## Agentic Appointment Engine Governance-first orchestration system for healthcare routing. Most organizations rely on infrastructure designed long before modern AI capabilities emerged. Effective AI deployment therefore requires systems capable of integrating with existing operational environments rather than replacing them entirely.
